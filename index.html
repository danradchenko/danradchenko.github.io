I'm currently a Hermann-Weyl-Instructor at the Institute for Mathematical Research at ETH Zurich.
From February 2022 I will be a CNRS research fellow working at Laboratoire Paul Painlevé at the University of Lille.

My research interests include modular forms, algebraic number theory, convex and discrete geometry, combinatorics, as well as approximation theory and Fourier analysis.
